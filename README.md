# NeuroFeed

A study-focused social learning platform that transforms your study material into a scrollable feed of short-form educational content.

Upload PDFs, slides, docs, or lecture audio — get reels, swipe cards, flashcards, quizzes, summaries, and an AI tutor grounded in your own material. Wrapped in XP, streaks, and daily goals.

> Make learning feel like scrolling TikTok and stick like flashcards.

## Status

Early development. MVP in progress.
